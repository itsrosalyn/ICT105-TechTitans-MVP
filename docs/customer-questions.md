# Customer Discovery Questions

## Project Title

QR code attendance system with GPS verification

## Target Respondent

Rangsit University students, Lecturers

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project about attendance management?
2. Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Tell us about the last time attendance was taken in your class or workplace.
4. What were you trying to do during the attendance process?
5. What happened from start to finish?

## Current Workaround

6. How do you currently record or manage attendance?
7. What tools, apps, or methods do you use for attendance tracking?
8. What is difficult, slow, confusing, or frustrating about the current attendance method?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

9. How often do attendance-related problems occur?
10. How serious is this problem on a scale of 1–5? Why?
11. What happens if attendance records are inaccurate, missing, or not updated properly?

## Closing

12. What improvements would you like to see in an attendance management system?
13. May we use your anonymized responses as part of our course project evidence?
