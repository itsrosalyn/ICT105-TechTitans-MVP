# Experiment Script

## 1. Opening Script

Thank you for helping us test our QR Code Attendance System prototype. This is **not** a test of you—we are testing whether our system and interface are easy to understand and use. Please think aloud while completing each task. Feel free to share your thoughts, questions, or any difficulties you experience.

---

## 2. Test Scenario

You are a student attending a lecture at Rangsit University. Your lecturer asks you to record your attendance using the QR Code Attendance System. You need to log in, scan the QR code, verify your location, and check whether your attendance has been successfully recorded.

---

## 3. Tasks for Tester

| Task No. | Task Instruction | Related Requirement | Expected Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| T1 | Open the login page and log in as a student. | FR-01, FR-02 | User successfully accesses the student homepage. |
| T2 | Scan the QR code for today's class attendance. | FR-03 | User completes the QR code scan successfully. |
| T3 | Allow GPS location verification. | FR-03, FR-10 | System verifies the user's location within the allowed classroom area. |
| T4 | Confirm that attendance has been recorded successfully. | FR-11 | User receives a confirmation message with attendance details. |
| T5 | View your attendance history and search for a previous attendance record. | FR-05, FR-06, FR-07, FR-08 | User can view, search, and understand attendance records and attendance percentage. |

---

## 4. Observation Notes

Record where the tester:
- Hesitates or becomes confused.
- Clicks the wrong button or page.
- Needs assistance to complete a task.
- Gives useful comments or suggestions.
- Encounters any errors or unexpected behavior.

---

## 5. Closing Questions

- Was the attendance process easy to understand?
- Was scanning the QR code and verifying your location straightforward?
- Were the confirmation message and attendance history clear?
- Which feature did you find most useful?
- What improvements would you suggest before the final prototype?
