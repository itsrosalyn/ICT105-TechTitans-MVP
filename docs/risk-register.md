## Overall Risk Decision

The prototype is safe to continue developing and testing as an MVP. Firebase Authentication, role-based access control, and time-limited QR codes reduce several security and privacy risks. However, the most significant remaining risk is that physical attendance cannot yet be verified because GPS validation is currently represented only as a prototype workflow. Before deployment beyond classroom testing, the team should implement real GPS verification, review Firestore security rules, and provide clear information about how attendance data is collected and stored.
