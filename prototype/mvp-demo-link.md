## Testing Notes
1. Open the login page and sign in as either a student or lecturer.
2. If testing as a lecturer:
Navigate to the Lecturer Dashboard.
Select Generate QR Code to create a session QR code.
Keep the generated QR code visible for students to scan.
3. If testing as a student:
Log in and navigate to the Home page.
Click Open Scanner.
Scan the QR code displayed by the lecturer.
Enter the required information in the attendance popup (if prompted).
Complete the GPS verification process.
Click Confirm Check-In.
4. After successful check-in:
View the Attendance History page to verify that the attendance record has been added.
Return to the Lecturer Dashboard to view updated attendance information.
